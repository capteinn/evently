Event description

Description

Come along to the Yogyakarta premiere of documentary film 'Aceh: beyond the tsunami'

Selasa, 19 Desember 2017

Waktu: 18.30 - 20.30

Lokasi: Auditorium IFI - LIP Yogyakarta

Tiket gratis, silahkan daftar

(Tickets are FREE, but please register to reserve your seat.)

Post screening Q&A with the Australian filmmakers.

The film is 70 mins in Acehnese & Indonesian with English subtitles.

*******************************************************

'Aceh: beyond the tsunami' is a documentary exploring the extraordinary stories of survivors of the 2004 Indian Ocean tsunami in the province of Aceh, Indonesia where approximately 170,000 lives were lost.

Through the vivid recollections of 10 Acehnese survivors, 'Aceh: beyond the tsunami' explores processes of grieving, healing and rebuilding. The film sheds a unique light on how the Acehnese responded to their tremendous loss with a renewed religious devotion, becoming the only province in Indonesia to formally practice Shariah law. Discover how the tsunami has transformed this deeply conservative yet rapidly modernising region.
